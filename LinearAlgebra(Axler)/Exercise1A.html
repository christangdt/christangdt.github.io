                                    1

                                    Suppose a and b are real numbers, not both 0. Find real numbers c and d such that

                                    1/(a+bi)=c+di

                                    Solution: We have

                                    1a+bi=a−bia2+b2=aa2+b2−ba2+b2i

                                    thus c=a/(a2+b2) and d=−b/(a2+b2).

                                    4

                                    Show that α+β=β+α for all α,β∈C.

                                    Solution: write α=a+bi and β=c+di where a,b,c,d∈R, then

                                    α+β=(a+bi)+(c+di)=(a+c)+(b+d)i=(c+a)+(d+b)i=(c+di)+(a+bi)=β+α
